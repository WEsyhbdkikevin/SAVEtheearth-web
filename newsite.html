The purpose of making this

We aim to raise awareness and promote actions to save our planet.

Protect our earth with our heart and self.

We want to remind you to always take care of the earth for long-term sustainability so that our earth remains healthy and clean and also has good and well-maintained environmental results.

Have good habits with

  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
  • Menghemat listrik dan energi
  • Menjaga alam dan tumbuhan
  • Memakai air dengan secukupnya
  • Tidak membakar hutan dan lahan hijau
